WPC ETA Approvals

The National Radio Regulatory Authority, or WPC, is in charge of policing the radio spectrum. To meet the demands of users of wireless technology, WPC Office grants the WPC License (ETA) for electronic and IT goods.

  • Full documentation support

Extended Producer Responsibility

ERP Registration

Every maker of Electrical and Electronic Equipment (EEE) has extended producer responsibility (EPR). This is to ensure the correct treatment and disposal of e-waste by directing it to a licensed dismantler/recycler.

  • Basic / State / Central license
  • Product category approval
  • Label design consultation
  • Annual renewal support
